# Acceptable Use Policy

Last Updated: 2026

This Acceptable Use Policy outlines appropriate ways to use Hawk™ within Discord servers.

## Intended Use

Hawk™ is designed to provide logging and moderation support for Discord communities.

The bot should only be used for legitimate server management purposes.

## Prohibited Activities

Users may not use Hawk™ to:

- Violate Discord's Terms of Service
- Conduct harassment or targeted abuse
- Attempt to exploit or disrupt the bot's systems
- Perform automated abuse or spam activities

## Enforcement

Servers or users found violating this policy may lose access to Hawk™ without prior notice.

The developer reserves the right to restrict access in order to maintain service integrity.
